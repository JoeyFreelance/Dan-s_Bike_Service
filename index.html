About

Dan’s Bike Service was started in 2019 and built from a genuine passion for cycling. Whether it’s a commuter bike, a family bike or a race machine, every bike gets the same care and attention.

I believe in honest advice, transparent pricing and quality workmanship with no surprise bills. Every job starts with an assessment and if anything else is discovered during the repair, it’s always discussed first.

From puncture repairs to full rebuilds, Dan’s Bike Service is here to help keep you safely on the road and enjoying your ride.
